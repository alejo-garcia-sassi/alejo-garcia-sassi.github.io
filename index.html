About Me

I am a mathematician with broad interests in Dynamical Systems, Low-dimensional Topology, and Geometry, as well as their applications to fields such as Fluid Mechanics and Astrodynamics.

Currently, I hold a position as a Postdoctoral Researcher (FAPESP) at the Institute of Mathematics and Statistics, University of São Paulo (IME-USP). I am also a Junior Professor (on leave) at the Instituto de Matemática y Estadística Rafael Laguardia (IMERL), Universidad de la República (UdelaR).

I earned a joint PhD between USP and UdelaR (2024), under the supervision of Alejandro Passeggi and Fábio Armando Tal. During my doctoral studies, I completed three research stays at Sorbonne Université (IMJ-PRG), working with Pierre-Antoine Guihéneuf.
