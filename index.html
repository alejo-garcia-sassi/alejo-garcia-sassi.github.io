Summary

I am a mathematician with a broad interest in Dynamical Systems, Geometry, and Topology, as well as their applications to fields such as Astrodynamics and Fluid Mechanics.

Currently, I am a Postdoctoral Researcher (FAPESP) at the Institute of Mathematics and Statistics, University of São Paulo (IME-USP), and I hold a permanent position as Junior Professor at the Instituto de Matemática y Estadística Rafael Laguardia (IMERL), Universidad de la República (UdelaR), Uruguay.
